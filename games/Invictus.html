INVICTUS

A tactical VR Milsim platform built on unit cohesion and combined arms gameplay. Deploy with your squad and fight through dynamic combat scenarios.

Overview

Invictus is a VR Milsim platform built around cooperative gameplay, realism, and strategic combat. The experience combines immersive VR weapon handling with advanced simulated systems and interactive environments. Players deploy into combat scenarios ranging from full-scale operations to small-team raids to training environments, each contributing to the overall progression system.

Game Modes

Expedition EARLY ACCESS

The primary online experience. Expedition focuses on cooperative operations against hostile forces within an ongoing campaign. There are multiple modes for the Expedition experience, but all focus around the collective campaign.

Sandbox EARLY ACCESS

An open environment with developer-style tools. Spawn units, experiment with systems, and explore gameplay mechanics freely. Complete with multiple tutorial and trial-run events to expand your skillset and knowledge.

Holdout EARLY ACCESS

A wave survival mode where players defend against organized enemy forces in escalating combat scenarios. Progression and enemy wave intensity increases as players progress. Squad up and hold out as long as possible.

Zulu Hour COMING SOON

Deploy into abandoned, but contested territory to gather high-value intel. Explore collapsed regions, gather supplies, complete objectives, and extract before the one-hour window closes.

Tasking COMING SOON

Short tactical missions playable solo or cooperatively that support the wider war effort. Smaller-scale operations intended for players looking to have the milsim experience at a shorter timeframe at an off-pace mode.

Economy & Equipment

Invictus features a persistent equipment economy tied to mission performance. Completing operations, multiplayer missions, and deployments rewards players with operational funds. You are awarded cash to spend on your personal kit and attachments, while Supply Points are used for cooperative efforts such as deploying vehicles, building or repairing structures, and more.


These funds can be used to purchase equipment from the armory, including:

  • Weapons
  • Attachments
  • Plate carriers
  • Tactical gear
  • Operational equipment

The primary multiplayer mode, Expedition, uses faction-restricted loadouts to maintain authenticity and balance between forces. Other modes allow broader equipment freedom for experimentation and custom loadouts.
